Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar and choose whether you're a student or organization. Fill in your details and verify your email address to get started.

How can I search for internships?

Use the search bar on the homepage to find internships by keywords or company name. You can also browse by category from your dashboard and apply filters to match your preferences.

How do I apply for an internship?

Once you've found an internship you're interested in, click "Apply" and submit your resume and a cover letter. You can track your application status from your dashboard.

Can I save internships to apply later?

Yes! Click the bookmark icon on any internship listing to save it. Your saved internships will be available in the "Saved" section of your dashboard.

How do organizations post internships?

Organizations can sign up through the Sign Up page, select "Organization," and complete their profile. Once verified, they can post new internship opportunities.

Is there a fee to use InternConnect?

No, InternConnect is completely free for students to browse and apply to internships. Organizations may have optional premium features.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team at internships@knust.edu.gh or use the contact form on this page. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Can I modify my application after submission?

You can edit your application details before the organization reviews it. Once reviewed, you'll need to contact us to make changes.

Still Have Questions?

Contact our support team - we're here to help!
